About NEXT:

Welcome to NEXT, a student-run agency at Humber College where creativity meets real-world challenges. Our talented students collaborate with industry leaders to deliver fresh, innovative solutions across marketing, advertising, PR, and design.

Our Mission Statement:

"At NEXT, we bridge the gap between education and industry, providing students with hands-on experience while delivering exceptional results for our clients."

Our History:

"Originally known as Building F, NEXT was launched in 2016 as an initiative by the Faculty of Media and Creative Arts at Humber College. Over the years, we have evolved into a dynamic agency driven by the passion and creativity of our students."
